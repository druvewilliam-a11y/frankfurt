The von Halberg Family Office is a discreet, independent private family office based in Frankfurt am Main. We provide tailored investment management, portfolio reporting, and family coordination services to a small number of multi-generational families.

Our focus is long-term capital preservation with a conservative risk profile.

The von Halberg Family Office maintains a deliberately low digital profile to protect the privacy of our client families. We do not engage in public marketing or active social-media activity.
